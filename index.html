ABOUT US

DOP Industry is a creative studio of web developers and graphic designers offering premium digital services at incredibly flexible pricing. Established on January 1st, 2025, we’re committed to making high-end design and development accessible to everyone — starting at just ₹200.


Our services range from sleek, mobile-friendly websites to captivating graphics tailored for individuals, small businesses, and startups. Whether you're building your first website or leveling up your brand identity, we’ve got your back — without blowing your budget.


Our core team includes talented developers and designers working under creative codenames: Erx, Juvo, Nive, and Clix — each driven by innovation, quality, and transparency.

At DOP Industry, our mission is clear: deliver modern, responsive, and visually stunning websites and graphics at prices that make sense. No inflated costs, no compromises — just clean, scalable results that work.


As we grow, our vision stays grounded: expand our reach to serve more individuals and businesses, without ever raising our prices.
